seg_filterlen

synopsis

filters segments on length

syntax

    seg=SEG_FILTERLEN(segments,minlen)
    seg=SEG_FILTERLEN(segments,minlen)
    [seg,i]=SEG_FILTERLEN(...)

description

seg=SEG_FILTERLEN(segments,minlen) returns the segments that have at least a length of minlen.

seg=SEG_FILTERLEN(segments,minlen) returns the segments that have at least a length of minlen and at most a length of maxlen.

[seg,i]=SEG_FILTERLEN(...) also returns the indices of the filtered segments into the original segments list. I.e. seg=segments(i,:)
