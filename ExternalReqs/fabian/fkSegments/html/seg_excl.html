seg_excl

synopsis

logical EXCLUSIVE operation on segment lists

syntax

    seg=SEG_EXCL(seg1,seg2,...)

description

seg=SEG_EXCL(seg1,seg2,...) peforms the logical EXCL operation on the segments passed in. The segments in seg2 and up are excluded from those in seg1. I.e. the operation is: seg1 AND NOT [seg2, seg3,...].
