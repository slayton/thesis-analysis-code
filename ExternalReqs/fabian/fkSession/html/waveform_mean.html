waveform_mean

synopsis

calculate waveform mean

syntax

    [wavemean,wavestd]=WAVEFORM_MEAN(ttfile,id)
    [...]=WAVEFORM_MEAN(ttfile,id,maxchan)
    [...]=WAVEFORM_MEAN(ttfile,id,maxchan,center)

description

[wavemean,wavestd]=WAVEFORM_MEAN(ttfile,id) calculates the mean and standard deviation of the spike waveforms in the tt-file for the spikes records indicated by the id vector. The waveforms are centered at the peak of the waveform in the channels with the largest peak before calculating the mean. The peak is centered at the 10th sample.

[...]=WAVEFORM_MEAN(ttfile,id,maxchan) uses the specified channel for finding the peak for centering.

[...]=WAVEFORM_MEAN(ttfile,id,maxchan,center) centers the waveform at specified sample.
