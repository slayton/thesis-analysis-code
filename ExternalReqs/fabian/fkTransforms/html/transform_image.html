transform_image

synopsis

apply transformation to image

syntax

    [timg,tx,ty]=TRANSFORM_IMAGE(img,angle,scale,offset)

description

[timg,tx,ty]=TRANSFORM_IMAGE(img,angle,scale,offset) returns the transformed image, which has been rotated around its center by angle degrees, scaled by a factor of scale and translated by offset. Both scale or offset can be scalars for uniform transformation in x and y dimensions or two element vectors for non-uniform transformation in the two dimensions. The output is the transformed image timg, and the x and y extents of the new image.
