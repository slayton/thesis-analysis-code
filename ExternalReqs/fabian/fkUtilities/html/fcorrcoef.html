fcorrcoef

synopsis

correlation coefficient of two functions

syntax

    r=FCORRCOEF(f,g)
    r=FCORRCOEF(f,g,limits)
    r=FCORRCOEF(f,g,limits,tol)

description

r=FCORRCOEF(f,g) correlation coefficient of the two functions f and g over the range [0 2pi].

r=FCORRCOEF(f,g,limits) returns the correlation coefficient over the specified limits.

r=FCORRCOEF(f,g,limits,tol) sets the tolerance for the integrating quad function.
