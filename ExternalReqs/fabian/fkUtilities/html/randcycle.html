randcycle

synopsis

randomly cycles columns or rwos

syntax

    m=RANDCYCLE(m)
    m=RANDCYCLE(m,dim)

description

m=RANDCYCLE(m) randomly shifts and cycles columns of matrix m.

m=RANDCYCLE(m,dim) randomly shifts and cycles along specified dimension.
