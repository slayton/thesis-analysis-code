roundto

synopsis

round numbers

syntax

    m=ROUNDTO(m)
    m=ROUNDTO(m, f)

description

m=ROUNDTO(m) round m to nearest integer.

m=ROUNDTO(m, f) round m to nearest multiple of f.

example

roundto( [3.4 4.8] ) %returns [3 5]
roundto( [3.3 5.7], 0.25 ) %returns [3.25 5.75]
